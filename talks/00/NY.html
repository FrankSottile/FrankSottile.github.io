AMS 2000 Fall Eastern Regional Meeting in New York, New York 4-5 November 2000

Title: Elementary Transversality in the Schubert Calculus.
Session Name: Special Session on Algebraic Geometry

Author: Frank Sottile

Abstract: We give an elementary and characteristic-free proof that general codimension-1 Schubert varieties meet generically transversally in a Grassmannian and in some related varieties. This implies that the corresponding intersection numbers computed in the Chow (and quantum Chow) rings of these varieties are enumerative in all characteristics.
