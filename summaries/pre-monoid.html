Bergeron, Nantel; Sottile, Frank
A monoid for the Grassmannian Bruhat order. (English)
[J] Eur. J. Comb. 20, No.3, 197-211, Art. No.eujc.1999.0283 (1999). [ISSN 0195-6698]

If $S_\infty$ denotes the group of permutations on the natural numbers fixing all but a finite number then $u\in S_\infty$ may be used to define Schubert polynomials $S_u\in Z[X_1,X_2,\dots]$ as a homogeneous basis indexed by these. For $u,v\in S_\infty$, the product $S_uS_v= \Sigma_w c^w_{uv}$ $(w\in S_\infty)$ where the structure constants $c^w_{uv}$ are positive integers whose (collective) interpretation and (combinatorial) construction is of interest, even if not completely transparent. In this useful paper a special partial order, the Grassmannian Bruhat order (GB-order), on $S_\infty$ is considered further with an eye to progress in the description of the structure constants via a special monoid $M$ analoguous to the nil-Coxeter monoid for the weak order on $S_\infty$ defined via relations obtained from the GB-order having desired properties because of special characteristics of this order (and similar ones) whose derived abstract properties for descriptions of constants $c^I_\lambda$ such that $c^w_{uv(\lambda,\kappa)}= c^{wu-1}_\lambda$ for unique Grassmannian permutations $v(\lambda,\kappa)$ such that $c^w_{uv(\lambda,\kappa)}\ne 0$, where $c^w_{uv(\lambda,\kappa)}= 0$ if $u\nleq w$ in the $k$-Bruhat order associated with $v$.
