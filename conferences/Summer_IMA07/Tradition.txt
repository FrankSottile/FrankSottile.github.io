Some features of the Tradition dorm


Parking:   There is covered parking available.

Pool:  There is a swimming pool.   Bring a bathing suit to 
         enjoy a break from the heat.

Internet:  There is internet access in the rooms.

Linen Service:   Sheets, blanket, pillow, towels including bath,
                 washcloth, and face towel are provided as well as
                 toilet paper, shower curtain, and soap in these rooms. 

Personal Food Preparation:  Each room has a refrigerator and microwave.
                 There are also community kitchens.

Lighting:   No desk lamps!
