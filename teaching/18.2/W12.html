  • Now that I have gotten to this part of the course, I see that we do not do any coding theory (we are skipping Section 5.3.3, as there are no readings on codes).   • Also the reading about infinite sets. Apparently the author is Stanislaw Lem, a Polish science fiction writer whom I read as a teenager. This topic, the infinite hotel, is popular with students. It is also called Hilbert's Grand Hotel, or simply the Hilbert Hotel.
  • Finally, a tiny bit of deductive reasoning in Chapter 8.
