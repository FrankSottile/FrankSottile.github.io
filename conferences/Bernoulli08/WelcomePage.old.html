Real and Tropical Algebraic Geometry

From January through June 2008, the Bernoulli Center of the EPFL is hosting a
research program on the topic of Real and tropical algebraic geometry.

The main themes of the program are
• Tropical geometry
• Gromov-Witten invariants and enumerative tropical geometry
• Welschinger invariants and enumerative aspects of real algebraic and symplectic geometries
• Topology of real algebraic varieties
• Bounds on real solutions
The program will consist of short courses and regular seminars throughout the term, punctuated by three workshops and a final conference.
January 21--26, Introductory Workshop
March 10--15, "Tropical and symplectic aspects of real geometry"
April 21--26, "Enumeration and bounds in real algebraic geometry"
June 16--21, Final conference
