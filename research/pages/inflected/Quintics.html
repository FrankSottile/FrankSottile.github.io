5. Maximally Inflected Quintics

    Since 9=3(5-2), a rational plane quintic has 9 ramification points, counted with multiplicity. The table below gives names for the different types of ramification points possible for quartic curves, together with the lowest order terms (germ) in a parameterization of the ramification. View the pictures of the germs.

Name Index Partition Germ
Flex (0,1,3) 1 (s,s3)
Cusp (0,2,3) 11 (s2,s3)
Planar (0,1,4) 2 (s,s3)
Claw (0,2,4) 21 (s2,s4)
Box (0,3,4) 22 (s3,s4)
Flat Flex (0,1,5) 3 (s3,s5)
Beak (0,2,5) 31 (s3,s5)
  (0,3,5) 32 (s3,s5)
  (0,4,5) 33 (s4,s5)
( The Term `Box' refers to its Young diagram, a better term should be found)
