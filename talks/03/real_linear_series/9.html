Maximally Inflected Quintics
There are 42 plane quartics with 9 given flexes. Here they are at the points -8, -5, -2, -1, 1, 2, 5, 8, and infinity. This set is stable under x to -x on P1. In these pictures, this amounts to reversing the parametrization and reflecting the curve in the vertical axis. Symmetrical curves are fixed under this involution, and asymmetrical curves come in pairs. Altogether, there are 6 symmetrical curves and 36 assymetrical curves with this choice of ramification. (In fact, this decomposition of 6 and 36 persists for any similar symmetric (under x maps to -x) choice of the 9 points of ramification.
