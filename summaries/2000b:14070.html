Sottile, F. (3-TRNT)
Pieri's formula via explicit rational equivalence.
Canad. J. Math. 49 (1997), no. 6, 1281--1298.
Pieri's formula for the Grassmannian manifold describes the intersection multiplicities for a Schubert cycle and a special Schubert cycle. This formula also determines the expansion into Schur functions of an arbitrary Schur function times a Schur function indexed by a single row. In this paper the author presents a new proof of Pieri's formula by explicitly describing a sequence of deformations (inducing rational equivalence) that transform a generic intersection of a Schubert variety with a special Schubert variety into a union of distinct Schubert varieties. One of the main features of this construction of rational equivalences is that it exhibits a branching structure that parallels Schensted insertion and therefore other combinatorial algorithms for Pieri's formula.
