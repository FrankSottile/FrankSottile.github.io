Add citation to clipboard ------------------------------------------------------------------------ 2001e:14054 14N15 (14M15 14P99) Sottile, Frank(1-MA) Real Schubert calculus: polynomial systems and a conjecture of Shapiro and Shapiro. (English. English summary) Experiment. Math. 9 (2000), no. 2, 161--182. ------------------------------------------------------------------------ Summary: "Boris and Michael Shapiro have a conjecture concerning Schubert calculus and real enumerative geometry which would give infinitely many families of zero-dimensional systems of real polynomials (including families of overdetermined systems)---all of whose solutions are real. It has connections to the pole placement problem in linear systems theory and to totally positive matrices. We give compelling computational evidence for its validity, prove it for infinitely many families of enumerative problems, show how a simple version implies more general versions, and present a counterexample to a general version of their conjecture." ------------------------------------------------------------------------ Return to List Item: 6 of 21 «First Last» ------------------------------------------------------------------------ (c) 2001, American Mathematical Society
