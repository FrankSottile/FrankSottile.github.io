Orientability of real toric varieties

By Evgenia Soprunova and Frank Sottile.
    We characterize the orientability of an abstract real toric variety as well as the orientability of a toric subvariety of a sphere. We also determine the number of components of the smooth locus of a toric variety. These results are proven for an extension of the Davis-Januskiewicz notion of a small cover to singular spaces. We characterize the orientability of toric varieties associated to posets, and discuss an application to the study of real solutions to systems of polynomial equations.
