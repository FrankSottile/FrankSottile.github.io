Accommodations, Facilities, and Reception.
Accommodations The hotel where we expect to lodge the students and lecturers is Adebayo Akande Hall, which is located just north of the main Campus of the University of Ibadan, about three kilometers from the mathematics department. It is privately-run, but the land belongs to the Univesity of Ibadan. It is not in the town proper. The hotel is relatively new.

Exterior of Hotel
Interior Courtyard. Note the benches.
There are three types of rooms that we plan to use.
1 Double Occupancy Room, for the Research School Participants. We have been quoted a price of 5,000 Naira/night for each room.
2 Single Occupancy Room for lecturers. We have been quoted 8,000 Naira/night.
3 Double Occupancy Suite. For families. We think this will be about 13,000 Naira/night.

The seats. 		This lecture hall is light and airy—we could use the natural light. This would work for lectures using a board (note two white boards). It is likely too bright for a lecture with a beamer. Also, it needs repairs to its seats. This should be not hard to get done right.

The other class rooms in the department are darker, and could work for beamer talks, but may be too dark for lectures. Most of them (shown below) have seating for 50, which may be too few seats for the school. There is one which is both of intermediate brightness, and seats more than 50.

The will be rooms in the building under construction that may be adequate.
