Some Combinatorial Hopf Algebras

Marcelo Aguiar
Nantel Bergeron
Aaron Lauve
Frank Sottile
Abstract Here, we archive some MAPLE files we used in our general study of Combinatorial Hopf Algebras [ABS03], as well as our study of the Malvenuto-Reutenauer Hopf algebra [AS02] and of the Hopf algebra of planar binary trees [AS03]. This also contains numerous examples of the structure of these objects.
