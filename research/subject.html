Frank Sottile's Homepage.

The core of my work concerns various aspects of the Schubert calculus, which was my original interest. Over time, these interests have broadened somewhat. A rough classification of my work breaks it into either combinatorics or geometry. A finer classification is Schubert calculus, Combinatorial Hopf Algebras, Real Algebraic Geometry, Computational Algebraic Geometry, Applicable Algebraic Geometry, and Computational Geometry.
