(Proposed) SIAM Activity Group in Algebraic Geometry

  Proposal for a new professional society, within SIAM, devoted to applications of algebraic geometry,
     in particular including MEGA.
  Application will be discussed at SIAM meeting in early July.
  If approved, will start now/or January 2010.

  Activities of this group will include:
       Inaugural conference Autumn 2011 (pre-planning stage).
       It will be your society, so the activities are up to you !
