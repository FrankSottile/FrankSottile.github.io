Directory listing of 7flexes1planar.maple/

These files were used to draw the maximally inflected quintics with 7 flexes and one planar point found at ../7flexes1cusp.html. The most important file is the precomputed parameterizations of the curves. We used a combination of Maple and Singular to compute them, and also the data concerning the double points. 
  data                    7 Kb    Mon Mar 25 19:48:39 2002
The precomputation of this data was accomplished by a suite of Maple and Singular files (unfortunately all undocumented).
