Real Hessian Curves

Adriana Ortiz-Rodríguez, and Frank Sottile.

We give some real algebraic plane curves whose hessians have more connected components than had been known previously. In particular, we give a quartic whose hessian has 4 compact connected components (ovals), a quintic whose Hessian has 8 ovals, and a sextic with 11 ovals and 2 unbounded components. We discuss the possible configurations of ovals of hessian curves and of parabolic curves for surfaces arising as graphs of polynomials.
