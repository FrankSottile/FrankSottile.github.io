Zometool 120-cells in Nigeria 

Frank Sottile, Saturday 26 July 2014

      In follow-up activities to lectures on "Platonic solids in the next dimension", in trips to Nigeria, I have worked with small groups of students to build projections of the 120-cell in R3. (The model at left resides in at the Usmanu Danfodio University in Sokoto and dates from July 2012.)
      My page from the original construction in Sokoto from 2012 is worth a view. Below are pictures from all three constructions, Sokoto in July 2012, Ibadan and Ilorin, both in July 2014.
