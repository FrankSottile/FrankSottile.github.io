News:
  • Over 60 TAMU mathematics uundergraduates took the 86th annual William Lowell Putnam Mathematical Competition on December 6, 2025. Two students, Mark Shiliaev and McKinley Xie, finished in the top 200, and Joshua Im in the top 500. The Texas A&M team finished in first-place finish in the MAA Texas Section (a first for them).
  • Texas A&M Computer Science and Applied Mathematics Senior Montgomery Bohde was selected as a 2026 Churchill Scholar. He is the first every Churchill Scholar from Texas A&M University.
