Advising

    Mathematics is a subject with a rich history that rests upon the insight of those who have come before. For it to have a bright future, we need to identify and nurture the talents of the next generation. I believe very strongly in this part of my job, and work to help those younger than I to acheive their goals.

    This takes many forms; from collaborating with students and postdocs to running enrichment activities and making presentations to students at all levels.

    The most important and direct form though, is through advising students and postdocs. This page is dedicated to that task.
