Distribution of Frobenius elements in the Galois group for the 27 lines

Elements of E6 have 20 conjugagcy classes as elements of S27. For each, we display the logarithm of the difference between the empirical (observed) fraction and
the actual fraction in E6 (vertical axis) and the logarithm of the corresponding prime. The green polygon is the negative logarithm of the square root of the expected number of Frobenius elements.

It is a measure of the expected sensitivity. The data points (circles) are blue when the empirical fraction exceeds the fraction in E6, and red when the empirical fraction is less than the actual fraction.
