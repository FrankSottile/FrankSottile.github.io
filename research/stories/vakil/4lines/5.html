The problem of four lines in P3




In the fourth stages of the specialization sequence, nothing happens to either solution lines.

For the blue solution, this is the case (yes,no) in Table2/Figure 8 of GLR, and for the magenta solution, it is a case of (no,no). The movement of the leftmost red checker for the blue solution reflects the moving point falling into the fixed point.
