Common Transversals and Tangents

Frank Sottile
University of Masssachusetts, Amherst
19 July 2002
Symbolic Computational Algebra 2002
University of Western Ontario 		1
    We use symbolic computation to study sets of lines meeting k fixed lines that are tangent to 4-k spheres in R3, called common transversals and tangents.

    Besides the interesting geometry, computation, and pictures, the geometry in 3-space is a fertile area for potential applications of symbolic and computational algebra.

       
Joint work with Gabor Megyesi and Thorsten Theobald.
