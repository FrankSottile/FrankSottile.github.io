Degenerate Configurations 10
II. For which arrangements of k lines and 4-k spheres in R3 are there infinitely many common tangents and transversals?
1 line and 3 spheres: Only degenerate configurations exist. (with Megyesi).
 
2 line and 2 spheres: Besides degenerate configurations, finitely many, highly symmetric configurations exist. (with Megyesi and Theobald).
