Coamoeba of a plane in 3-space
    The coamoeba of the plane x+y+z+1=0 in (C*)3 consists of those triples (θx, θy, θz) for which there exist a quadrilateral whose sides have these angles.
In the fundamental domain [-π, π]3, the closure of this coamoeba is the cube with an open rhombic dodecahedron removed.
